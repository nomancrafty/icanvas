Common Questions

How quickly can you produce?
Most projects ship within 3-7 business days, depending on volume. We provide exact timelines during project planning.
What if items arrive damaged in shipment?
In the rare event an item arrives damaged, our team will partner with you to resolve the issue quickly and ensure your project stays on track.
Can we use our own artwork?
Absolutely — custom production is a major part of our B2B service. We handle everything from file preparation to final production.
Do you work with procurement teams?
Yes — we handle documentation, invoicing, and compliance requirements for corporate procurement processes.
Do you help with design selection?
Yes — your project includes a dedicated design consultant who works with your team to select and curate artwork.
